# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects respectful, direct, and professional collaboration.

Participants should:

- assume good intent
- give technical feedback without personal attacks
- be clear about tradeoffs and uncertainty
- respect boundaries around privacy, security, and disclosure

Participants must not:

- harass, insult, or intimidate others
- publish private information without permission
- derail technical discussion with abusive or discriminatory behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that violates this policy and may limit participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.

## Reporting

If you encounter a conduct problem, contact the maintainers through the repository’s private contact channel when available. Do not escalate personal disputes in unrelated issue threads.
